Google System Design Interview Preparation Guide:

  1. Understand the Basics: Make sure you have a strong understanding of basic system design concepts such as distributed systems, databases, APIs, etc.
  2. Study Past Questions: Look at past system design questions asked in Google interviews to understand the type of problems you might be asked to solve.
  3. Practice with Mock Interviews: Use websites like Pramp or Interviewing.io to practice system design interviews with other engineers.
  4. Read Google's Documentation: Google has a lot of public documentation about their technologies and architecture. Reading these can give you insights into how Google designs its systems.
  5. Learn from Others: Read engineering blogs and watch videos to learn how other engineers have approached system design problems.
